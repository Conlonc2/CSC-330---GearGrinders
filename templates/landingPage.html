About Us

At InternREQ we aim to provide a platform that connects applicants and employers. Sponsors post internship opportunities to a communal job board where qualified applicants can apply. Applicants can search for internships matching their interests, gain valuable experience for their résumé and possibly start a career. Simply put, our goal is to efficiently enable sponsors to post their unfilled internships and allow applicants to find and apply for them.

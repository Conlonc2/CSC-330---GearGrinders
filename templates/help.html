Welcome to InternREQ! We hope you enjoy our website. How do I register?

Toward the right side of the landing page, there is a box that contains the Login and Registration buttons(Figure 1)

The buttons under the Registration heading labeled Faculty, Student and Employer will redirect you to the registration page. If you're having trouble finding the buttons or they appear to not be functioning, you can also reach the registration page by clicking this hyperlink Registration

To know if you are on the right page, compate your screen to Figure 2.

All users will enter their First Name and Last Name into the relevant fields. Then comes a very important option, the Account Type selector.

Clicking the drop down arrow will allow you to select whether you are creating a Student, Employer or Faculty account. Here you pick the option corresponding to the account you're looking to create

For the sake of our tutorial, we are going to create a Student named Joe Green. Continue to fill the forms and when you're finished, review the information before submition. NOTE: For Student accounts, the email must be a SouthernCT email address. See Figure 3 for an example

After you have finished entering your information, push the 'Submit' button at the bottom of the page.

Upon successful submission, the following should appear on your screen(Figure 4).

The Dashboard
How do I navigate to my profile?
Upon being brought to the dashboard, click on the 'Links' button on the navbar to show what pages are available to you.

Clicking on the 'Profile' link, you will be brought to your profile page which will display information pertaining to your account. Figure 1 shows an example.

How do I edit my profile?

Once you are on your profile page, there will be an 'Edit Profile' button in the top right corner.

Clicking this button will bring you to the Edit Profile page with relevant fields filled with your current information(See Figure 2)

This is where you may go and fill in information pertaining to your user role. As the account used for this FAQ is a student account, these fields are what students will see.

Once you have properly filled out the corresponding fields, you can press the 'Submit' button the bottom to finalize your changes.

The submitted changes will then be reflected in your profile page(See Figure 3 for updated profile).
